排程產 Sequence

指令 rake sequence
檔案 lib/tasks/sequence.rake
邏輯 1. 移除當天的 sequence
2. 查詢當天需建立 sequence 的 pre_campaign： 
SELECT `pre_campaign_dailyreach`.*
FROM `pre_campaign_dailyreach`
INNER JOIN `pre_campaign_detail`
  ON `pre_campaign_detail`.`id` = `pre_campaign_dailyreach`.`pre_campaign_detail_id`
INNER JOIN `pre_campaign`
  ON `pre_campaign`.`id` = `pre_campaign_detail`.`pre_campaign_id`
WHERE `pre_campaign_dailyreach`.`c_date` = '2023-07-07'
  AND `pre_campaign_detail`.`enable` = 1
  AND (`pre_campaign_detail`.`pack_id` IS NOT NULL)
  AND `pre_campaign`.`status` = 'oncue'
  AND `pre_campaign`.`has_sequence` = 1;
3. 建立 sequence
